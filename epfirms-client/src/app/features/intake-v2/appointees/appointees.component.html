Appointees

Please provide the executor, trustee, power of attorney, and medicial power of attorney for any estate planning documents that are being drafted. The system makes assumptions to limit intake time. First assumption is that the spouse will be the executor, trustee, POA, and MPOA. However, you can override the assumption by entering a different person in the trustee section.
